SEAGATE DELIVERS 24.92% RETURNS TO CONTAINER INVESTORS
ralph 27 Dec 2021 95 Views 2 Comments Business, Warehouse

Seagate, a premier international alternative investment company announced today an average Return on Investment of 24.92% to investors who own containers in 2017.

Seagate exceptional performance follows the wave of growth across the logistics industry. Notably, the Chinese government has invested half a trillion U.S. Dollars modernizing the country, working to connect 80% of the country's cities by high-speed railway. This government investment ensures clients of Seagate will enjoy sustainable returns, due in part to the fact that railways take decades to build. And at a US Dollar-per-kilometer cost of more than $19 million, the government's commitment to building 33,000 kilometers of high-speed railway means tremendous growth and investment opportunity.

As part of the explosion of infrastructure construction, the government is building 97 new domestic airports and 28 nuclear power stations. Considering that 96% of materials used in building railways, buildings, bridges and roads are moved in a shipping container, the long-term opportunity and viability associated with shipping container leasing is a magnet to both private and institutional investors.

"We are well-positioned to capitalize on container leasing investment opportunities for our clients," said, Chelsea Zhang, Director of Marketing at Seagate. "The industries we serve are exploding, and the Chinese government's massive investment illustrates just how important and profitable it is to invest in containers now."

Jacques Piccard, Managing Director for Seagate, added, "Our track record of outperforming the financial markets showcase the opportunity. The returns are paid monthly. As hard assets, containers guarantee capital preservation for the container owners." He continued saying, "The industry is ripe for investment growth right now."

ABOUT SEAGATE:

Seagate is here to help your investments run smoothly and successfully; from sourcing shipping containers, to leasing them, right down to transferring your income every month ultimately simplifying the entire investment process for you. Our strong track record over the years allows us to identify the most profitable markets for container deployments to maximize investors' returns.
